# Sales-Dashboard
Welcome to my Sales Dashboard. 

## Introduction

In this project, I will dive into a large sales dataset to extract valuable insights. I will explore sales trends over time, identify the best-selling
products, calculate revenue metrics such as total sales and profit margins and create visualizations to present your findings effectively.

## Purpose

To analyze sales data to identify trends, top-selling products, and revenue metrics for business decision-making.

## Objectives
1. Explore Trends Over Time
2. Identify Best-Selling Products
3. Calculate Key Performance Metrics (Revenue, Product Orders, and more)
4. Create Impactful Visualizations

## Tools used

1. Power Query for data wrangling
2. Microsoft Power BI for building the dashboard

## Key Insights

-The month of January 2019, had the lowest sales followed by a gradual increase up to April. There was a decrease in total sales from April reaching another low point in September. October had a sharp increase in sales and dropped again in November. December had the highest sales.

-The Macbook Pro Laptop is the best-selling product with 8.04 million in sales revenue. iPhone came second with 4.79 million in revenue.

-San Francisco ranks as the best city in terms of total sales followed by Los Angeles.

-The fourth quarter had the highest sales percentage with 33.49%
